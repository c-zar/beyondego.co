Main Services

1 on 1 Training

Get coached by me directly in person or through web call. I will teach you what exercises to do, and how to perform them properly. In addition, I’ll constantly be assessing limitations or weaknesses you might have in your mobility, and muscles.

Online Coaching

Follow an individualized workout program optimized for your goals. You can access the workouts and communicate with me regularly through the TrueCoach application

Guided Meditation

Learn to master your body through your mind. I can teach you how to become aware of your thoughts and emotions, and gain a new perspective of them. I also teach how to break free of limiting or negative beliefs, and reprogram positive beliefs.
